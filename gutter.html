For new eavestrough installation

The DoublePro® Microfiltration continuous hanger™

The DoublePro® Microfiltration continuous hanger™ is built to stand up the worst weather conditions in Canada and keep your gutter free of ice and debris year after year. This product is covered by a 360° lifetime warranty on materials, durability, and clog-free performance! DoublePro is the only gutter guard with a clog-free warranty that covers pine needles. Welcome to the most extensive warranty in the industry!


Alu-Rex

T-Rex® continuous hanger™

The T-Rex® continuous hanger™ system fastens securely to your home, making it 50% stronger than traditional systems such as spikes or hangers. The continuous fastening system can support 250 lbs per linear foot! The T-rex protects gutters from damage caused by ice and snow, helps them perform optimally year round, and prevents debris from accumulating inside.
The T-rex comes with a lifetime warranty on it's sturdiness and materials, PLUS a clog-free 40-year warranty!

For existing eavestrough

The Gutter Clean System®

The Gutter Clean System® stops debris, leaves, buds, insect nests, and small animals from getting into your eavestroughs and protects your house from water damage. Like the T-Rex or the DoublePro it can easily handle 3 times as much water during the heaviest rainfalls on record. This gutter guard fits onto any kind of gutter, no matter the fastening system. It comes with a 40-year no clog guarantee, so you can be sure it won’t get clogged.
