Gutter Cleaning

At Home Service Solutions our goal is to take all your stress and worries away! When your gutters are not maintained it can cause some serious long-term problems to your home.Our gutters are meant to send water safely away from our homes and down to the sewer systems. If we don’t have our gutters regularly cleaned, debris clogs our gutters and causes overflow. When water overflows behind your gutters it can get trapped between your fascia and eavestrough. which can cause leaks, cracks in your foundation and even mold and mildew growth. Moss Removal

Having your roof moss free not only improves the beauty of your home or building, but extends the life of your roof. Moss thrives in environments that are damp and shaded which can severely damage the structural integrity of any type of roof. Over time moss will absorb the moisture in its environment and will grow roots that can lift up shingles. Once your shingles start to lift moisture can get into your home and lead to bacteria and mold growth. This eventually will lead to holes in your roof and leaks into your home.
Don't let your home become a victim to moss growth. Call us today to save your home.

Lawn Aeration

Have you ever driven by a house that has a perfect lawn and wondered how it looks so good?
The answer is Lawn Aeration.
Different seasons take a different toll on our grass. In the summer kids are running around and playing. In winter heavy snow is sitting on it for months! Over time our soil gets compacted and prevents grass from creating a healthy root system. When our soil is so compacted water and fertilizer can't reach the roots of the grass.

Aeration relieves soil compaction by opening up your lawn’s “pores” to allow water, nutrients and oxygen to reach the roots of your grass and create deeper, stronger roots.

Relax, the grass is always greener with us.

Driveway Sealing

Driveway sealing is a great way to protect you driveway and bring that brand new look back!
All foundations naturally need maintenance over time!
Our driveways hold a lot of weight every single day.

The main reason our driveways crack over time is water erosion. If you've ever noticed a small chip or crack in your driveway during the spring, summer or fall we strongly recommend having your driveway sealed before winter hits!
During the winter season water will find those small holes and because we live in Canada the water will eventually freeze. When water freezes it expands and causes those small chips and cracks to spread all across your whole driveway! Im sure we don't have to paint this picture but that will continue to happen year after year until the whole driveway needs to be replaced. That's a bill none of want to see.

Have you ever owned a car that leak oil? Yep, you guessed it, something as simple as an oil leak is going to cause deterioration to your driveway.

The good news is, by regularly having your driveway sealed, it will grab your neighbors eye, boost your curb appeal and protect your foundation for years to come.

Our team is professionally trained with commercial grade equipment to clean your driveway, remove all the weeds, edge a straight clean border and spray an even coat!

Relax, we've got it.

Concrete Sealing

When we buy a brand new car we are going to wash it, change the oil and maintain it regularly, we wouldn't think twice about it. Well, we poured concrete for a reason, to have a foundation that's strong, resilient and beautiful! Concrete needs to be washed and sealed every year to keep it in its glory. Remember, any little thing that sits on concrete for long enough can and will leave a stain. Sealing your concrete makes it more resistant to weather, grease, stains and it makes it much easier to clean!
If you're thinking this is a DIY, remember that sealers must be applied properly! A lack of experience and knowledge when it comes to applying your sealer can cause blemishes and bubbles.

Our team will take their time to scrub all the dirt off your concrete and seal it with your preferred colour and grade so the next time you are walking by you won't be able to take your eyes off of it.
